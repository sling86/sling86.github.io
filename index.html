About Contract Candles

Our team have been specialising in candle manufacture since 1997, producing handmade candles of the highest quality.

We offer a complete design and technical service to all our customers, ensuring that every aspect of development and production are of the highest standard. Our attention to detail and commitment to excellence, along with our strong customer relations are the reasons why some of the most prestigious high street brands, boutique stores, designers and perfumers have chosen us to provide them with premium candles at competitive prices.

The services we offer range from simple bulk production from free issue components, through to full bespoke contract manufacturing where we turn your idea into a beautifully packaged and competitively priced candle ready to sell.
