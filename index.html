Contract Candles

Specialists in bespoke premium candles since 1997, offering a full design & technical service. Producers of candles and diffusers for renowned luxury brands, designers and boutique stores.

More

Best of British

Our factory is located in the beautiful South Downs National Park on the Surrey/West Sussex border. We produce all our candles on site where they are hand poured to the highest quality standards. We work with some of the biggest luxury brands in the world so quality is second nature to us.

We are certified against ISO 9001:2008 for the provision of design, development, manufacture and packaging services of candles and reed diffuser products to meet own label and client specifications.

Design & Development

At Contract Candles we have an experienced design team which allows us to offer a fully bespoke packaging & glass service. The team will manage your project from start to finish taking the stress out of sourcing, sampling and finalising products.

Creating the perfect candle takes some considerable time and expertise. Our development team have just that expertise to ensure your candle burns beautifully, smells perfect and enhances any room. We carry out accredited in house testing on all our candles and diffusers giving you that extra peace of mind.

Next

Production & Packaging

Our production team run a well organised efficient, filling process for all our candles and diffusers. Every stage is checked thoroughly and monitored carefully. We offer complete traceability and batch testing to ensure every product that leaves our factory is of the highest standard.

The packaging team are highly skilled, with most products being hand finished. Every product is finished to your specific requirements. Quality control is key in this area and where we feel we stand apart from our competitors.

Factory Shop

Our factory shop is located on site and offers a large selection of quality candles at factory outlet prices. Some come under our own brand Lower Lodge Candles and can also be bought online lowerlodgecandles.com. There are also plain glass candles at bargain prices and great deals on bulk boxed candles.

Complimenting this, is a variety of candle & home accessories. Perfect for a spot of retail therapy and an ideal place to look for gifts for all occasions.

In addition to our Fernhurst factory shop, we also have a store in Horsham which also has the same bargains.
